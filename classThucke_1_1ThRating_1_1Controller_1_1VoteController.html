initSignalSlotDispatcher (   $slotName)
protected

Initialize signalSlotHandler for given action Registered slots are being called with two parameters

  1. signalSlotMessage: an array consisting of 'tablename' - the tablename of the rated object 'fieldname' - the fieldname of the rated object 'uid' - the uid of the rated object 'currentRates' - an array constising of the actual rating statistics 'currentrate' - the calculated overall rating 'weightedVotes' - an array giving the voting counts for every ratingstep 'sumWeightedVotes' an array giving the voting counts for every ratingstep multiplied by their weights 'anonymousVotes' - count of anonymous votings if the user has voted anonymous or non-anonymous: 'voter' - the uid of the frontenduser that has voted 'votingStep' - the ratingstep that has been choosen 'votingName' - the name of the ratingstep 'anonymousVote' - boolean info if it was an anonymous rating
